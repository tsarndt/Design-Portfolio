About Me

Hey there, I'm Tyler and thank you for visiting my online design portfolio!

After receiving my bachelor’s degree from Iowa State University in 2012 (has it really been that long?), I kicked off my landscape architecture career in sunny California. For four years I worked on urban park design, academic campuses, master plan streetscape redevelopments and high-rise luxury tower courtyards. In 2015 I discovered a new avenue to use the design skills and software programs I learned in landscape architecture – website design and front-end application development!

I took a deep dive into the world of front-end development by hard-coding this very portfolio site with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Since then I have explored additional front-end languages, honed my skills with content management systems, and gained valuable insight into definitive SEO practices. These learning experiences have led to a number of projects including personal websites, online blogs and professional sites for small businesses.

The crossover of skill sets between landscape architecture and front-end development has been broader than I would have ever imagined. Within this website you will find an archive of landscape architecture projects that formed the graphic design strategies, problem-solving techniques and project management skills I use every day in web development. If you have any questions or comments about my work feel free to shoot me a message; I can be reached best by email, LinkedIn, or bat signal... hey, it's worth a shot!
